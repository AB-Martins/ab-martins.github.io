    I am doing my PhD in informatique on the ENS de Lyon as a member of the MC2 group of the LIP. My research interests include structural graph theory and graph coloring problems.

    Contact: beatriz.martins@ens-lyon.fr.

    Curriculum Vitae

    I have a degree in Mathematics and a master's degree in Computer Science, both from Universidade Federal do Ceará, from 2018 to 2024 I was a member of the ParGO group. During my master's degree, I worked on the Harmonious Coloring problem under the supervision of Júlio Araújo and Marcio Santos. Currently, I'm doing my PhD under the supervision of Nicolas Trotignon.
