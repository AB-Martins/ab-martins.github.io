I am a PhD student at ENS de Lyon and member of the MC2 research group of LIP. My research interests include structural graph theory and graph coloring problems.

Contact: beatriz.martins [at] ens-lyon.fr

Curriculum Vitae

I have a bachelors degree in Mathematics and a master's degree in Computer Science, both from Universidade Federal do Ceará (UFC). From 2018 to 2024 I was a member of the ParGO research group. During my master's degree, I worked on the Harmonious Coloring problem under the supervision of Júlio Araújo and Marcio Santos. Currently, I am doing my PhD under the supervision of Nicolas Trotignon in the characterization of \((\theta, C_{3})\)-free graphs.
